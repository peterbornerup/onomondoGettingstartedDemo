To get started simiply push the button on the device provided by Onomondo and watch the magic happen!

What's being transmitted?

As the button is pushed the device wakes up from deep sleep and measures the remaing battery capacity and checks the signal quality reported by the SIM800 modem. The process is repeated five times and the data is sent in individual JSON formatted packages. The signal quality can be decoded using the table found here but as a rule of thumb anything over 10 is acceptable.
